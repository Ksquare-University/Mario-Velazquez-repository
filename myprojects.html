Vision Robot

Development of an automated validation and storage system by recognizing parts based on their shape, color, area, size, and position.

The system is based on the use of a band of metal pallets that will carry in them one by one the pieces to be stored and that will pass through a vision cell where there will be a cell phone camera that will be linked to a computer that will perform the recognition of the part parameters and that it will send information to a PLC for storage on a shelf in the position determined by its characteristics.

Guitar Tuner

The automatic guitar tuner stands out for tuning the guitar precisely without having to touch the pegs, also, it is user-friendly because it has an interface that indicates the status of the operation. Compared to other products, this does not need a mobile application for its operation since the signal from the guitar is connected directly to the device.

The tuning time depends on the initial position of the pegs, on average it takes 15 seconds for each string, the noise does not cause a problem since it has a filter which is responsible for eliminating the signals that are not provided by the guitar.
